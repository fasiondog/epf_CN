主要描述

Scrum projects make progress in a series of "sprints". Sprint are analogous to iterations. The typical duration of a sprint is 2-4 weeks.

Scrum项目依赖于一系列的“冲刺（sprint）”获得进展。“冲刺”类似于迭代。 一个sprint的典型持续的时间是2 - 4周。
