任务：Sprint Retrospective Sprint回顾会议
Following the Sprint Review, the team gets together for the Sprint Retrospective.

在Sprint评审会议后，团队成员一起召开Sprint回顾会议
规程：Scrum Activities Scrum活动
用途

The Sprint Retrospective is the main mechanism for taking the visibility that Scrum provides into areas of potential improvement, and turning it into results.

Sprint回顾会议是使潜在的改进领域可见，并将其转化为成果的主要机制。

关系
角色主执行者:
  • Scrum Team Scrum团队
其他执行者:
  • Product Owner 产品负责人
  • ScrumMaster Scrum主管
主要描述

The Sprint Retrospective is an opportunity for the team to discuss what's working and what's not working, and agree on changes to try.  One approach taken is that the whole team gathers and discusses what they’d like to

  • Start doing
  • Stop doing
  • Continue doing

Sprint回顾会议是一个团队成员讨论什么应该被保留、什么应该被抛弃和对改变和尝试达成一致意见的一个机会。该途径收集和讨论整个团体将要：

  • 开始做什么
  • 结束做什么
  • 继续做什么
关键注意事项

The Scrum Team Scrum团队, the Product Owner 产品负责人, and the ScrumMaster Scrum主管 will all attend, and a neutral outsider will facilitate the meeting.

Scrum Team Scrum团队，Product Owner 产品负责人和ScrumMaster Scrum主管需要参加该会议，并且一个保持中立的局外人会有利于促进会议。
